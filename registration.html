Registration Process:
Check Course Schedule: Review the course schedule to find the classes you wish to register for. Make note of the course codes, timings, and prerequisites.

Meet with Advisor:
Meet with your academic advisor to discuss your course selections and ensure they align with your academic goals and degree requirements.

Access Registration System:
Log in to the university's student portal or registration system to access the registration tool. Select Courses:
Use the registration tool to search for and select the computer science courses you wish to enroll in. Enter the course codes and select the desired sections.

Check for Conflicts:
Review your course selections to ensure there are no scheduling conflicts or overlapping class times.

Submit Registration:
Once you have finalized your course selections, submit your registration through the online system. You may need to confirm your choices before the registration deadline.

Payment:
After registering for courses, ensure that you pay any applicable fees or tuition charges by the specified deadlines to complete your registration process.

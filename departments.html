DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at KUMASI TECHNICAL UNIVERSITY, where we are dedicated to excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. Our department offers a dynamic and interdisciplinary environment that fosters creative and scientific exploration in the field of computer science.

Academic Programs: Our department offers a comprehensive range of academic programs designed to equip students with the theoretical knowledge and practical skills necessary for success in the rapidly evolving tech industry.

Undergraduate Programs: Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Computer Science, including specializations in Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, and Software Engineering.

Graduate Programs: Master of Science (M.Sc.) and Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Computer Science, offering advanced study and research opportunities in emerging areas such as Data Science, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision.

Faculty Expertise: Our faculty members are distinguished scholars and industry professionals who bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to our department. They are actively engaged in groundbreaking research and are committed to providing high-quality education to our students.

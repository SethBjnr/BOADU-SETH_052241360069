COMPUTER SCIENCE PAYMENT DETAILS

COMPUTER SCIENCE PAYMENT
Welcome to the Computer Science department's payment page. Below, you'll find important information and instructions on how to make payments for various services and fees related to computer science courses and activities.


Tuition and Fees:
Tuition:
Tuition fees for computer science courses vary depending on factors such as residency status, degree program, and credit load. Please refer to the university's tuition schedule or student accounts office for specific tuition rates.

Lab Fees:
Some computer science courses may have additional lab fees to cover the cost of materials, software licenses, or equipment usage.

Technology Fees:
Technology fees may be assessed to cover the cost of maintaining computer labs, software licenses, and other technological resources.

PAYMENT METHODS:

Online Payments:
Make payments online through the university's student portal or payment gateway using credit/debit cards or electronic funds transfer (EFT). Ensure that you have the necessary account information and follow the instructions provided on the payment platform.

In-Person Payments:
Visit the university's cashier's office or student accounts office to make payments in person. Accepted payment methods may include cash, check, money order, or credit/debit cards. Payment Plans: Explore options for payment plans or installment payments if you are unable to pay the full amount at once. Contact the student accounts office for assistance with setting up a payment plan.



PAYMENT DEADLINES:

Fall Semester:
Payment for full semester tuition and fees is typically due by 4th MAY 2024.

Spring Semester:
Payment for spring semester tuition and fees is typically due by 30th MARCH 2024.

Summer Semester:
Payment for summer semester tuition and fees is typically due by 27th MAY 2024.

Late Fees:
Late payment may result in additional fees or penalties, so be sure to pay on time to avoid any disruptions to your enrollment or registration status.


FINANCIAL AID AND SCHOLARSHIP:

Financial Aid:
If you have been awarded financial aid or scholarships, ensure that your awards are applied to your student account to cover tuition and fees. Contact the financial aid office if you have questions about your award package or eligibility requirements.

Scholarships:
If you have been awarded scholarships specific to computer science or STEM fields, verify that your scholarships are applied correctly to your student account.
